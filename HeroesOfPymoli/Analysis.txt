Written description of three observable trends based on the data.
1. In-game purchases are far more likely to be made by men.
2. Players in the 20 to 24 age group spend 2.7 times more on in-game purchases than any other age group. 
3. Players in the 34 to 39 age group tend to spend more in single transaction than any other age group.
4. Three out of five of the most popular items are also the most profitable.
